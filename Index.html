Smart BSF Rearing Cage

Operation & Technical Manual

System Description

This automated system is designed for high-efficiency Black Soldier Fly (BSF) cultivation. Using an ESP32 and Raspberry Pi 5, the cage monitors environmental parameters to optimize larvae production through predictive modeling.

Rearing Cage & Components

DHT22 / SHT31: Monitors ambient Temperature and Humidity.
BH1750: Measures Light Intensity (Lux) for mating stimulation.
Active Low Relays: Controls 12V Heaters, Fans, and 5V Humidifiers. (Note: Logic is reversed—LOW is ON).
DS18B20: Waterproof sensor for substrate temperature.

Solar Power Setup

100W Solar Panel: Positioned for maximum PV intake.
30A SCC (B2 Setting): Configured for 3s6p Lithium-ion battery protection.
12V DC LED: Manual/Automated lighting for night-time checks.
Critical Safety Note: Always ensure the DC Breakers are accessible. The 12V 100Ah battery provides the primary buffer for 24-hour operations.

System Startup Procedure

STEP 1: Connect the Battery to the Charge Controller first. (Wait for display to activate).
STEP 2: Connect the Solar Panel to the Charge Controller.
STEP 3: Switch on the Load Breaker to power the ESP32 and Sensors.
